Symbols of Our Country

symbols are the things that represent our country

Examples of American Symbols:

  • Our flag is out most famous symbol, along with our colors of red white and blue. The strips represent the original 13 Colonies and the stars represent the 50 states.
  • The Great Seal of the United States was added in 1782 and is attached to important documents.
  • The national bird is the Bald Eagle. The bald eagle was chosen because it represents freedom.
  • The national anthem is the Star Spangled Banner. It was written by Francis Scott Key in 1814.
  • The statue of liberty was given to us by france to represent liberty and independence and is the first thing some saw when they first entered America.
  • The Pledge of Allegiance is a pledge to the flag by Americans. It is used to open up many ceremonies.

    I pledge allegiance to the flag of the United States of America,

    and to the Republic for which it stands, one Nation, under God, indivisible,

    with liberty and justice for all.
